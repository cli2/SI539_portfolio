Make

I have always loved handcraft since childhood, and built 1:1 scale temporary housing, timber structures, and interactive lighting installations. In the past two years, I experimented more with digital fabrication tools including robotic arms, 3D printer, and CNC mills. I seeks to bridge the physical objects and create the internet between them by the means of designing and making.
